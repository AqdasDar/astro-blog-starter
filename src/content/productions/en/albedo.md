*Albedo* is a poetic short film co-directed by Hamza Dar and Yacine Oubaki. With a runtime of 8 minutes, the film was screened at the short film festival "C'est too court !" in Yerres as part of the "Jeunes Pousses / Talents du Territoire" category.
